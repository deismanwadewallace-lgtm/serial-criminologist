Serial Criminologist

Public criminology. Evidence over anecdote.

Serial Criminologist is a public criminology platform led by Dr. Wade Deisman. Its purpose is to close the gap between what the evidence shows about crime and what the public has been told: to move past headlines and reaction toward causes, patterns, and responses that actually work.

The goal is not to react louder. It is to understand better and respond more intelligently.

The platform is being rebuilt and expanded for launch in Fall 2026.
